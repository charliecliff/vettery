What is Vettery?

Vettery is a 3 month program that provides housing, work space, and a community for people who have always thought about working remotely or living abroad.

Who is Vettery for?

Vettery is tailor-made for professionals who have gotten tired of the mundanity of clock-punching cubicle farms, environments designed to molify the middle-of-the-road managers and the whatever-works wage-slaves.

WheRe does Vettery Go?

The Vettery team hand pick and pre-emptibvely scout it's locations before deciding to host a cohort. The team focuses on a number of factors such as ease of travel, cost of living, and presence of beaches.
