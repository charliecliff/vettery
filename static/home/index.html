What is Vettery?

Vettery is a boutique 3 month program that provides housing, work space, and community for people who have always thought about working remotely or living abroad.

Vettery focuses on bringing these people together with a close-knit familial atmosphere, building a community of Digital Artisans around the globe.

Vettery is tailor-made for professionals who have gotten tired of the mundanity of clock-punching cubicle farms, environments designed to molify the middle-of-the-road managers and the whatever-works wage-slaves.

So how does it work?

Vettery strives to create a close-knit and freindly atmosphere that allows ample opportunity for our cohorts to become fully immersed in the local cultures of their host countries.

In order to ensure this, the Vettery Team carefully reviews each applicant's perferences, background, interests, and working-styles. This ensures that we are able to match our applicant's personality and goals, creating as unique and as enriching an experience as possible.

Vettery provides 90 day accomodations in each of our host countries, each of which is hand-picked and pre-emptively scouted by the Vettery Team. The Vettery Team focuses on a number of factors when evaluating a potential Host Cuntry, such as ease of travel, cost of living, and presence of beaches.

By the end of the program, we at Vettery hope that every member of our Cohorts have had an authentic opportunity to experience living in, not just traveling around, our Host Countires and that each member of our Cohorts form lasting personal and professional relationships with their Cohort.
